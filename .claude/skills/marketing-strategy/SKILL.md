# Marketing Strategy

Develop and maintain marketing strategy artifacts stored in `strategy/marketing/`.

## Prerequisites

Before creating any artifact, read:
- `strategy/competitive/` for competitor research (required input)
- `strategy/marketing/` for existing marketing docs
- `strategy/business/` for business context

## Frameworks

### STP (Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning)
Produces a positioning statement: For [target audience] who [need], [Product] is a [category] that [key benefit]. Unlike [alternative], we [differentiator].

### RACE (Reach, Act, Convert, Engage)
Audit content and channels against each funnel stage. Identify gaps.

### Content Strategy Pyramid
Strategic narrative at top, pillar themes in middle, individual content pieces at base.

### Message-Market Fit Canvas
Maps segments to pain points to messages to proof points to channels.

## Output Artifacts

Save to `strategy/marketing/`:
- `positioning.md` — Target audience, category, positioning statement, differentiators, proof points, competitive positioning
- `messaging-guide.md` — Voice/tone, messaging hierarchy, approved language (use/don't use table), boilerplate (one-liner, elevator pitch, full description)
- `content-calendar.md` — Quarterly plan mapping content types to themes to channels
- `channel-strategy.md` — Channel selection, approach per channel, resource allocation

## Gap Analysis

Compare marketing presence against competitors across: channel coverage, content topics, messaging angles, SEO/keyword gaps, social proof. Produce prioritized gap list with effort and impact estimates.

## Quality Standards

- Ground positioning in evidence — every differentiator needs a proof point
- Be specific: "Reduces setup from 2 hours to 2 minutes" beats "Saves time"
- Acknowledge trade-offs honestly
- Date all competitive claims
- Include `Last Updated` date and update trigger on every document
