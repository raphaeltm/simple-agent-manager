# Template: Competitor Comparison Post

Use this template when creating content that compares SAM to a specific competitor. Always read `strategy/marketing/messaging-guide.md` and the relevant competitor profile before writing.

## Structure

```markdown
# [SAM vs Competitor]: [Key Dimension]

## The Short Version
[2-3 sentence summary for people who won't read further]

## What [Competitor] Does Well
[Honest assessment of competitor strengths — builds credibility]

## Where SAM Takes a Different Approach
[Frame as different philosophy, not "better" — BYOC vs managed, etc.]

## Detailed Comparison

| Dimension | SAM | [Competitor] |
|----------|-----|-------------|
| Infrastructure model | BYOC | [their model] |
| Pricing | [ours] | [theirs] |
| AI agent support | [ours] | [theirs] |
| Self-hosting | [ours] | [theirs] |
| [key differentiator] | [ours] | [theirs] |

## When to Choose [Competitor]
[Honest — tell people when the competitor is the better fit]

## When to Choose SAM
[Our sweet spot]

## Try SAM
[CTA]
```

## Rules
1. **Lead with competitor strengths** — builds trust, shows we're not insecure
2. **Frame as different philosophies**, not better/worse
3. **Include a "when to choose them" section** — honesty converts better than FUD
4. **All competitive claims must be dated and sourced** (reference competitor profile)
5. **Use approved language** from messaging guide
